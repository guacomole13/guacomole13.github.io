# LLM Context for michaelomole.com

## About This Site
This is the portfolio website of Michael Omole, a filmmaker, writer, and creative director based in Los Angeles.

## Site Structure
- / - Home page with hero video and selected work
- /film - Film index with mosaic grid of projects
- /film/[slug] - Individual film detail pages
- /writing - Writing index with featured articles
- /info - About/contact page

## Content Types
- Films: Short films, music videos, and branded content
- Writing: Articles and essays (external links)
